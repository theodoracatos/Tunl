TUNL - Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

TUNL is a simple arcade game. This policy explains what data TUNL and its third-party services collect, and why.

Data collected directly by TUNL

TUNL itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its developer. Game progress, high scores, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

TUNL shows interstitial ads between game sessions using Google AdMob. If you own the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase, ads are disabled entirely.

On iOS, TUNL asks for your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before requesting personalized ads. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized using your device's advertising identifier.

If you are in the European Economic Area, UK, or Switzerland, TUNL shows a consent form (Google's User Messaging Platform) before requesting ads, letting you choose whether to allow personalized advertising.

AdMob may collect the following on our behalf:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS / Advertising ID on Android), only with your consent
  • Approximate device and location information
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

See Google's Privacy Policy and Google's User Consent Policy for details.

In-app purchases

TUNL offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit / Play Billing. Payment details are handled by Apple or Google and never reach TUNL's developer. TUNL only receives confirmation of whether you own the purchase, stored locally on your device.

Leaderboards (Game Center / Play Games)

If you sign in to Game Center (iOS) or Play Games (Android), your score and public gamertag/alias may be submitted to a leaderboard managed by Apple or Google, governed by their respective privacy policies. TUNL does not receive or store your Game Center / Play Games identity.

Children's privacy

TUNL does not knowingly direct personalized advertising or data collection at children under the age required by applicable law in your region. Where required, ad requests are made non-personalized by default.

Your choices / requesting data deletion

TUNL has no account system and stores no personal data on its own servers, so there is no TUNL-held data to delete. The only data described above (your advertising identifier, approximate device/location, and ad interaction data) is collected and retained by Google AdMob directly, under Google's own retention schedule described in Google's Privacy Policy. To stop this collection or request its deletion:

  • iOS: go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, and turn off tracking for TUNL. You can also reset your Advertising Identifier under Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.
  • Android: go to Settings > Google > Ads (or Settings > Privacy > Ads on newer Android versions), then select "Delete advertising ID" or "Reset advertising ID."
  • Purchasing "Remove Ads" stops all future ad requests, and with them, all further data collection by AdMob.

Changes to this policy

If TUNL's data practices change, this page will be updated with a new "Last updated" date.
