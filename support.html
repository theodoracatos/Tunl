TUNL - Support

TUNL is a hold-to-thrust cave flyer: hold to thrust up, release to fall. Collect coins to widen the tunnel and unlock power-ups; avoid stalactites, mines, and the tunnel walls.

Need help or found a bug?

Email info@schedly.ch and include:

  • Your device model and OS version
  • What you were doing when the issue happened
  • A screenshot or screen recording, if possible

Common questions

How do I remove ads?
Open Settings from the title screen and select "Remove Ads" to purchase. This is a one-time purchase that disables ads permanently on that device/account.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Open Settings and select "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID / Google account used for the original purchase.

Why do I see a tracking or ad-consent prompt?
TUNL shows Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt (and, in the EU/UK/CH, a Google consent form) before requesting ads. See the Privacy Policy for details. You can decline and still play with non-personalized ads.

My high score / leaderboard didn't update.
Make sure you're signed in to Game Center (iOS) or Play Games (Android) in your device settings, then relaunch TUNL.
